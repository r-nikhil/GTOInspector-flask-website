What are the hand categories shown in your website?

Hand Categories:

We’re using Tom Chamber’s classification to segregate PLO hands into 76 categories preflop.

Pairing:

  • Unpaired: No pair in hand (AKQ9)

  • Paired: One and only one pair in hand (AAT5)

  • Double Paired: Two pairs (KKTT)

Suitedness:

  • Single Suited: Only one suit in hand (KhTh8c9d, As8s9sTc)

  • Double Suited: Two suits in hand (Ts8c9s6c, KsKdQsQd)

  • Rainbow: No suit in hand (KsQdTc5h, 8s8d9c9h)
