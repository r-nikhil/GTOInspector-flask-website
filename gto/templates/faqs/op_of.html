What is overplaying or underplaying?

{% endblock %} {% block answer %}
Whenever the GTO move and Hero move are different for a particular spot, the hand is categorised as either an Overplayed or Underplayed mistake. The table below explains when the hand is categorised as Overplayed or Underplayed:
GTO Move Hero Move Conclusion
Call Fold Underplay
Call 3bet Overplay
3bet Call/Fold Underplay
Fold Call/3bet Overplay
