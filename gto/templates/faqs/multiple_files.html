What if I upload multiple files at the same time?

{% endblock %} {% block answer %}

We process files sequentially for every user. If you have enough hands remaining in your account, all your files would be processed and shown as a single report. If you don’t have enough hands remaining in your account, we would process only the first ‘x’ hands where x is the hands remaining in your account at the start of processing.
